MARPOL Annex I deals with the prevention of pollution by oil. It strictly applies to Oil Tankers. It requires double-hull construction for oil tankers under Regulation 13G. Bulk Carriers do not carry oil as cargo and do not fall under Annex I cargo regulations.

For Bulk Carriers, Port State Control (AMSA and Paris MoU) strictly targets structural integrity, specifically hatch cover watertightness, cargo hold corrosion, and the International Maritime Solid Bulk Cargoes (IMSBC) Code.

Fire safety on Container Ships is highly scrutinized under SOLAS Chapter II-2. Inspectors specifically target sluggish fire doors, compromised fire dampers, and emergency fire pumps failing to pressurize to the required standard.
